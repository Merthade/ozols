The OaKs Loyalty Program

Grow your rewards with every banking activity

What is OaKs?

OaKs is our new loyalty program that rewards you for the way you already use our banking services. Every time you make a payment, save, invest, or protect what matters — you earn OaKs coins that can be redeemed for real benefits.

✓

Earn automatically

OaKs coins are added to your balance for everyday activities — no extra steps needed.

☆

Level up your status

Progress through Base, Bronze, Silver and Gold levels. Each level unlocks new perks and exclusive rewards.

♥

Redeem freely

Use your coins on discounts, gift cards, experiences, partner offers, donations, or surprise mystery boxes.

↑

Track your progress

See exactly how each action contributes to your balance with a transparent transaction history.

How do I earn OaKs?

You earn coins for a wide range of banking actions:

  • Use the app and pay with your card
  • Save money and reach your financial goals
  • Use financing in a responsible way
  • Protect your assets and loved ones with insurance
  • Grow your money through investing

Who is it for?

OaKs is available to all our customers, completely free of charge. The more you use our services, the more you benefit — but even small everyday actions add up.
